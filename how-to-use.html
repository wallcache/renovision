How to Use Renovision

Transform any doer-upper property into a stunning visualisation of its potential. See what could be, not just what is.

The Process

1

Paste a Rightmove Link or Upload an Image

Simply paste any Rightmove property listing URL, and Renovision will automatically extract all the room images. Alternatively, upload your own photos directly.

2

Select the Room You Want to Transform

Choose which room image you'd like to renovate - whether it's a tired kitchen, overgrown garden, or dated living room.

3

Customize Your Vision

Use our intuitive toggles to select your preferred style, room type, colour scheme, flooring, and more. Each option helps guide the AI to create your ideal renovation.

üí° Pro Tip: Be Explicit with Extra Notes

The "Extra Notes" field is powerful - use it to be specific about what you want. For example: "Add a kitchen island with bar seating, install brass cabinet handles, include open shelving with warm wood tones"

4

Generate Your Renovation

Click "Generate Renovation" and watch as AI transforms the space in seconds. The AI preserves the room's structure while applying your chosen style and finishes.
